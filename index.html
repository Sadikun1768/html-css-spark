Who We Are

We are the best in the market

Welcome to Spark Event Solutions Ltd, London’s trusted partner for comprehensive hospitality and facility management services. We excel in full-scale hotel operations, providing expert support for housekeeping, kitchen management, front desk staffing, waiting staff, event support, and human resources. Our dedicated team ensures seamless operations while upholding the highest standards of service and hygiene. In addition to hotel management, we offer tailored facility management solutions for corporate, office, and household needs, including housekeeping, maintenance, and support services.
